Privacy Policy

Privacy for Charm AI.

Last updated: June 30, 2026

Data Collection

Charm AI does not require you to create an account. In-app preferences, favorites, history, language preferences, subscription status, and simple usage counters are stored on your device or in Apple's App Group storage.

Conversation Content

The conversation text you enter, screenshots you choose for OCR, and profile photos you select are processed only when you start an AI analysis, reply generation, opener, or profile coaching request. This content is not used for advertising, tracking, or third-party profiling.

AI Processing

User-selected text, screenshots, profile photos, and profile or bio content may be sent through a protected AI proxy to a production AI service to generate responses and analysis. Provider keys are not requested from users in release builds and are not shown in the app interface.

Keyboard Extension

The Charm keyboard processes active text context only when you choose to generate a reply. iOS Full Access permission is required to send AI requests from within the keyboard and to read local App Group credit or premium state. The keyboard does not continuously monitor what you type in the background.

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store infrastructure. Charm AI does not receive your card number or payment credentials. We may use an anonymous subscription or customer identifier through RevenueCat to manage premium access, restore purchases, and verify subscription status. This identifier and purchase history are not used for advertising or tracking.

Notifications

Notifications are optional. If you allow them, Charm AI may send reminders or daily tips. You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Charm AI is intended for users aged 17 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from users under the age of 17.
